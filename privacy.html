Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Revisent ("we," "our," or "us") provides a browser extension and dashboard that helps organizations understand and improve how their teams use AI tools. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Through the Browser Extension:

  • AI prompts: The text you type into supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot) before and after refinement.
  • Conversation context: Recent messages in your current AI chat session, used solely to improve prompt refinement quality.
  • File references: Names of files mentioned or attached in your AI conversations, used for data leakage detection. We do not collect file contents.
  • Usage metadata: The AI platform used, timestamps, whether a prompt was refined, and intent classification (research or clarification).

Through the Dashboard:

  • Account information: Email address and authentication credentials managed through our authentication provider.
  • Organization data: Organization name, member roles, and department assignments.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Prompt refinement: Your prompts are sent to the Anthropic API (Claude) to generate improved versions before you send them to an AI platform.
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated usage data is displayed to organization admins through the dashboard, including message counts, platform breakdowns, and activity trends.
  • Topic insights: AI-generated summaries of research topics and knowledge gaps across your organization, powered by the Anthropic API.
  • Data leakage detection: File names are analyzed to alert users and admins when potentially sensitive files are referenced in AI conversations.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL platform with row-level security policies. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to organization data is restricted to authenticated members of that organization, with admin-only access to analytics and sensitive settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Revisent:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Supabase Authentication, database, and serverless functions supabase.com/privacy
Anthropic Prompt refinement and topic summarization via API anthropic.com/privacy

When your prompts are sent to Anthropic's API for refinement or summarization, they are processed according to Anthropic's API data usage policy, which does not use API inputs to train models.

5. Data Retention

  • Prompts and messages: Retained for as long as your organization's account is active. Admins can request deletion at any time.
  • Summary cache: AI-generated summaries are cached for up to 24 hours and automatically refreshed.
  • Account data: Retained until account deletion is requested.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Export your data in a standard format.
  • Opt out of prompt collection by disabling the extension.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Organization Admin Access

If you use Revisent through an organization, your organization's admins can view aggregated usage analytics, topic summaries, and data leakage alerts. Admins cannot see the full text of individual prompts in the analytics dashboard — only truncated excerpts used for topic classification.

8. Children's Privacy

Revisent is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the extension or dashboard.
