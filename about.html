Our Story

Revisent started with a simple observation: recycling is confusing, and contamination costs everyone. Nayan and Chris met as students frustrated by how often recyclables ended up in the trash—and how little data existed to fix it.

We built Revi to solve that. What began as a campus project became a mission to bring AI-powered waste intelligence to facilities everywhere—from universities to MRFs to enterprise campuses.

Our Mission

Every year, billions of dollars are lost to contaminated recycling streams. We believe AI can solve this problem at scale — by detecting waste in real-time, educating users, and giving facilities the operational intelligence they need to run cleaner, more efficient operations.
